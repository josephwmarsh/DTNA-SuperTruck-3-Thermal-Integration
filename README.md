# DTNA-SuperTruck-3-Thermal-Integration
Thermal system design and coolant routing for the SuperTruck 3 hydrogen fuel cell program. 

## Project Overview
This repository documents my engineering contributions to the **SuperTruck 3 Program**, a U.S. Department of Energy-funded initiative to develop hydrogen fuel cell-powered heavy-duty sleeper cabs. Working within the Advanced Vehicle Systems department, I delivered manufacturing-ready designs for both the "B-Sample" prototype and the "Final Demonstrator" vehicle.

## Major Projects

### 1. Aluminum Evaporative Cooling Tank (Final Demonstrator)
* **Objective:** Redesign a high-cost plastic evaporative cooling tank into a welded aluminum assembly to reduce cost and lead times.
* **Engineering Solutions:** Utilized standard off-the-shelf (COTS) components, including McMaster-Carr elbows and plugs, to simplify the supply chain.
* **Results:** Achieved a **$300 per-unit cost reduction**.
* **Documentation:** Produced a full technical package consisting of 12 sub-part GD&T drawings (ASME Y14.5) and a comprehensive Bill of Materials (BOM).


### 2. B-Sample Upfit Coolant Routing
* **Problem:** Identified a 3-4 mm interference between the primary coolant lines and the chassis crossmember.
* **Technical Execution:** Used the **Siemens NX Routing** application to re-path lines around the fuel tank.
* **Constraints:** Successfully maintained a **70mm minimum bend radius** to prevent tubing failure and ensured a **6mm hood clearance** for auxiliary components.


### 3. Radiator Baffle Design & Optimization
* **Task:** Engineered 6 TPV anti-recirculation baffles to optimize airflow through the radiator stack.
* **Timeline:** Completed full CAD and design release within one week to meet a critical program "Design Freeze," preventing schedule slippage.

## Portfolio of Subsystem Components (Minor Projects)
Beyond major systems, I was responsible for the rapid prototyping and design release of several critical support components:
* **Flowmeter Brackets:** Designed custom mounting solutions for 38mm and 50mm flowmeters.
* **Pump Mounting Bracket:** Developed full CAD models and engineering drawings for cooling system pump integration.
* **Battery Cooling Surge Tank:** Conducted initial concept development and fitment analysis for high-voltage battery thermal management.
* **Double-Ended Barbed Pipe Fitting:** Modeled custom fluid connectors for non-standard hose interfaces.

## Technical Skills & Software
* **CAD & PLM:** Siemens NX (Solid Modeling, Sheet Metal, Routing, Drafting, FEA).
* **Standards:** GD&T (ASME Y14.5) for manufacturing-ready documentation.
* **Collaboration:** Coordinated with Chassis (Sensor Placement) and Airflow specialists to resolve multidisciplinary design conflicts.
